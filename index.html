Hello Kyiv!

Magnificent place ever!

Kyiv is an important industrial, scientific, educational and cultural center of Eastern Europe.
It is home to many high-tech industries, higher education institutions, and historical landmarks.
The city has an extensive system of public transport and infrastructure, including the Kyiv Metro.

About Kyiv

  • Founded - AD 482 (officially)
  • Area - 839 km2
  • Population (1 January 2019) - 2,950,800

3 historical facts

  1. Kyiv is one of the oldest cities of Eastern Europe
  2. The city's name is said to derive from the name of Kyi, one of its four legendary founders
  3. Kyiv-Mohyla Academy was one of the oldest academic and theological schools amongst the Orthodox Christian countries of Eastern Europe and was found in 1615
